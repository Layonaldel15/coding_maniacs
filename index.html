About Us

Coding Maniacs is a club started as an initiative by the students from the department of Computer Science Engineering,Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning,Data Science and IT Department of Annamalai University.The goal of the club is to team up,work on cool projects with people of their own interest and to build a strong knowledge and leadership and teamwork among students.The club consists of budding Web developers,App developers,Machine Learning,Data Science and IOT Enthusiasts in addition to Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security Enthusiasts.
